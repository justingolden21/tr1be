Mission


TR1BE is a community of gamers focused on delivering a consistent supply of rigorous competition, positive mindsets, and a safe / welcoming environment.Our focus is nurturing the growth of a community that ensures the constant pursuit of the optimal gaming experience with our vessel being kickass events.At Tr1be, we prioritize creativity and individuality and this stands at our forefront through a steady feed of art and media catered to the interests and demands of the community that aspires to reach further. We aim to exist without interfering with any and all local community builders just like us! We stand firm in the belief that with the rapid expansion of eSports and competitive gaming it should be the local communities that dictate how that environment looks and feels. Whether you’re a new player or an established brand ambassador for your community, we invite you to join us and ensure the future of eSports serves the best interest of us and our fellow players! Our Events

Bear Knuckle Brawl – Located in Mira Mesa, this event is focused on comfort and a more casual competitive experience. Our all ages event offers an inviting transition into the world ofcompetitive gaming and plenty of other activities for the whole group! Come play some board games, eat delicious food, and adults can experience some of the best craft beer San Diego has to offer! We also made sure to offer a comfortable space to separate from the potentially exhausting social experience for our attendees that need a second to recharge.

Cero Muertes – Located in Imperial Beach, this event was designed with our neighbors from the south in mind. This event is sheltered by Kanai at The Salty Frog who jumped at the opportunity to create an awesome environment for competitive gaming! Grab a beer and tear it up at this 21+ Event! Expect tasty local flavors at an affordable price so you can game all night.

Dogfight – Located in Mira Mesa at Little Miss Brewery, this event was a necessary addition to our lineup as a way for our community to give back to the Veterans that have served and enabled us the ability to nerd out and pursue the life we choose. At TR1BE we have a strong connection and respect for our active duty and veteran gamers and this is our opportunity to give back. Drop by and experience amazing craft beers at an insanely low price with provided food (while supplies last)!

Interested in Hosting a Gaming Event?

At TR1BE we utilize detailed analytics and research to strategically position events that compliment the local gaming community and contribute something of value that can exist harmoniously. It is our policy that we only work with local businesses and community members that are focused on not only improving their sustainability, but are also attentive to the betterment of the communities they belong to. We communicate directly with other tournament organizers and the local community to ensure a cool concept doesn’t threaten the structure of what already exists. Our infrastructure and events prioritize sustainability and the best interest of the community that attends them. While it may be tempting to just throw together an event because your venue or business has a great space for it, we strongly advise communicating with us or a community ambassador to find the best fit for your prospective tournament.
